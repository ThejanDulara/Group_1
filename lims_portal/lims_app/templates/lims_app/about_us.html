History

TJK Library was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the need for a dedicated community space for learning and literacy. TJK Library has grown to become a cornerstone of the community, offering a vast collection of books and resources, educational programs, and community events.

Why We Started

We started TJK Library to bridge the gap between knowledge and the community. Our founders, Thejan Dulara, Janani Halwala, and Kabilashika Thiyagarajah, envisioned a place where people of all ages could come together to explore, learn, and grow. Their commitment to education and community service drives our mission and vision every day.

Vision

To be a beacon of knowledge and learning, fostering a community of informed and empowered individuals.

Mission

To provide access to a diverse collection of books and resources, promote literacy, and support lifelong learning through a welcoming and inclusive environment.
